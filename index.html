Telefónica Movistar – Revenue Analytics

Business Problem

Finance teams lacked centralized analytics for monitoring billing performance and collections efficiency.

Data Pipeline

Billing data extracted from operational systems → transformed with Power Query → modeled using a star schema → visualized in Power BI dashboards.

Key Metrics
  • Collection Rate
  • Aging Balance
  • Revenue Performance
  • Outstanding Receivables
Business Impact

Enabled executives to monitor revenue recovery trends and improved decision-making across finance operations.
