Telefónica Movistar – Revenue Analytics Platform

Business Problem

Finance teams lacked centralized analytics to monitor billing performance, collection efficiency, and outstanding receivables.

Data Pipeline

Billing and financial transaction data extracted from operational systems, transformed with Power Query, modeled with a star schema and analyzed using Power BI.

Architecture

Operational DB → Data preparation → Semantic model → Executive dashboards

Key Metrics
  • Collection Rate
  • Aging Balance
  • Revenue Recovery
  • Outstanding Receivables
Business Impact

Enabled finance executives to track revenue recovery trends and improved operational decision-making across collections teams.

Deloitte – Payment Reconciliation Automation

Business Problem

Manual reconciliation processes created delays and risk of reporting inconsistencies.

Solution

Developed automated reconciliation dashboards integrated with SQL stored procedures and .NET processing scripts.

Key Metrics
  • Payment reconciliation accuracy
  • Transaction mismatch detection
  • Daily financial reconciliation tracking
Business Impact

Reduced manual reconciliation workload and improved reliability of financial reporting.
